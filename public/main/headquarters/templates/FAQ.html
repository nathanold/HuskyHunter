Add A Clue

This should be used by people at our HQ. HQ can add newly decoded clues and input the locations.
After submitting a clue, it will be auto-assigned to the best field team.

Settings

This is where HQ can fine-tune certain aspects of the game. For example, this is where HQ would add a new Set of clues
once they were discovered.

Field Team 1-3

The 1st active group of players somewhere in Boston. The field team's region can be dynamically changed by control.
This pane will display a map of all current waypoints within the team's region, as well as a list of the tasks
(completed clues) submitted by Control. Each field team will be able to tap on the task they're currently completing
and be prompted to upload a photo. This photo will automatically be filed in the correct spot (by set number and clue number).

Unassigned Tasks

Tasks that do not fall under any field team but need to be completed. Some examples from last year are snapchat challenges
and challenges that took place on campus. This section will still support the same photo upload functionality that
field teams do (It's basically just a 4th field team).

Completed Tasks

A list of all completed tasks.
